Privacy Policy for Unmask Lab

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Unmask Lab ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our facial feature extraction application.

Key Point: Unmask Lab processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Access

To provide our facial feature extraction functionality, the App requests access to the following device features:

  • Camera: Required to capture photos for facial feature detection and extraction.
  • Photo Library (Optional): Only required if you choose to export photos from the app's gallery to your device's Photo Library. All captured photos are stored securely within the app's private storage by default.

2. Facial Feature Processing

Unmask Lab uses Apple's built-in AVSemanticSegmentationMatte technology to detect and extract facial features including:

  • Skin regions
  • Hair
  • Teeth
  • Glasses

Important: All facial feature processing occurs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device machine learning frameworks. No facial data, biometric information, or images are ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage

All photos and extracted facial feature images are saved within the app's private sandboxed storage on your device. This means:

  • Photos are stored securely within the app and cannot be accessed by other apps
  • You can optionally export photos to your Photo Library for sharing
  • We do not upload any images to external servers
  • We do not store any data in cloud services
  • We do not create backups of your photos on our systems
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all photos stored within the app

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any of your information with third parties. Since all processing is done locally on your device, there is no data to share.

5. Third-Party Services

Unmask Lab does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App operates completely offline after installation.

6. Data Retention

Photos are stored within the app until you delete them through the app's gallery. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all photos stored within the app. Photos you export to your Photo Library will remain there until you delete them separately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Permissions: You can grant or revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time through iOS Settings.
  • In-App Photos: You can view, export, and delete any photos through the app's built-in gallery.
  • Export: You can export photos to your device's Photo Library for sharing or backup.
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all photos stored within the app. Photos exported to your Photo Library will remain.

8. Children's Privacy

Unmask Lab is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Security

Your data security is ensured by Apple's iOS security architecture. All photos and processing remain within your device's secure storage. We have no access to your device or its contents.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
